Do you work with small local businesses?

Yes. Valencia is designed to support small businesses, clinics, stores, offices, and service companies that want stronger systems without a bloated process.

Can you help if we need both a website and automation?

Yes. Many projects combine website improvements, lead capture, booking flows, and internal workflow automation into one coordinated rollout.

Do you only work in Saskatchewan?

Valencia is based in Saskatchewan and works with businesses locally as well as across Canada depending on the service and setup requirements.

Can you support physical technology setup too?

Yes. That includes POS modernization support, CCTV planning, networking, Wi-Fi, and broader IT consultancy for business systems.

What if I am not technical?

That is exactly who the experience is built for. Recommendations stay grounded in practical business value, and explanations stay clear and direct.
