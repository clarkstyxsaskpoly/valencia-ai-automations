Process

A calmer, more structured way to deliver websites, automation, and IT projects.

Valencia keeps projects grounded in discovery, clear planning, realistic implementation, and practical support after launch.

Four-Step Model

Discover. Design. Build. Launch.

01. Discover

Review goals, pain points, existing systems, customer flow, and where time is being lost.

  • Clarify priorities and constraints
  • Map current tools and processes
  • Identify the highest-value first move

02. Design

Translate the business problem into the right structure, whether that is a website, an automation, or a broader system setup.

  • Plan content and service positioning
  • Shape workflows and interactions
  • Align scope with practical outcomes

03. Build

Implement with clean execution and a strong focus on reliability, usability, and handoff clarity.

  • Build site pages and service flows
  • Configure tools and automation logic
  • Test before launch

04. Launch & Support

Go live with confidence and keep momentum through support, iteration, and next-stage recommendations.

  • Launch review and adjustment
  • Operational support where needed
  • Future-ready next steps
